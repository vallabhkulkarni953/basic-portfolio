Best Epics You Should Know About

Epics are most important part of Literature

Mahabharat - By Maharshi Vyas

Mahabharat is an Epic that teaches way of life and polytics which is relevant in everyone's life.

Ramayan - By Maharshi Valmiki

Ramayan is an epic that focuses on the life of God Rama and teaches us the limits and dharma.

Bhagwad Geeta - By Maharshi Vyas

Bhagwad Geeta is a dialogue between Lord Shri Krishna and Pandav Arjun that teaches to work hard without expecting the output.
